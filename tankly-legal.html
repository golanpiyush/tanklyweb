Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built around the features hobbyists actually ask for — and a few they didn't know they wanted.

📊
Multi-parameter graphs
View pH, alkalinity, calcium, and nitrate overlaid on one chart. The #1 most-requested missing feature in competing apps — we built it from day one.
🐠
Species database
1,400+ species with care requirements, compatibility info, and temperament data. Tap once to add to your tank. Rare species? Add manually.
🌊
Living ripple feedback
Our signature UI moment: a water ripple plays when parameters are in safe range. Pulses red when out of range. No other app does this.
⏰
Smart reminders
Water change, filter cleaning, parameter testing — all auto-scheduled and logged with timestamps when marked done. Home screen widget shows what's next.
📸
Tank timeline
Dated photo journal becomes a visual month-by-month evolution of your tank. The emotional hook that keeps users coming back — and sharing on Reddit.
☁️
Cloud sync & export
Pro users get cross-device sync, full cloud backup, and CSV/Excel export. Your data is yours — always. We never lock parameter logging behind a paywall.
