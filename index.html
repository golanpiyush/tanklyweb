How it works

Simple by design.
Powerful inside.

Three steps from download to a fully synced, AI-aware aquarium companion.

1

Everything saves locally first

Log parameters, add livestock, run the app entirely offline. All data is stored on-device instantly — no internet required, ever.

2

Sign in to unlock the cloud

Google Sign-In connects your account to Firebase and Supabase. Streaks, community, and (for Pro) all your tank data sync automatically.

3

New device — instant restore

Sign into any new phone. Tankly checks your plan, pulls your tanks and history from the cloud, and you're back in seconds.
