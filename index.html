How Tankly sync works

1

Your data, always saved locally first

Everything you do in Tankly — logging parameters, adding livestock, setting up tanks — gets saved instantly on your device. No internet required. The app stores all your data locally so it's always available, whether you're online or not.

2

Sign-in unlocks cloud backup

Once you sign in with Google, Tankly connects to two cloud databases running in the background. Your streak and community profile sync for everyone, but your full tank data — parameters, history, livestock, equipment — only backs up if you're on a Pro plan. Free users still get their first tank backed up automatically as a safety net.

3

Switching devices is seamless

When you sign into a new device, Tankly pulls your data back down from the cloud in the right order — it checks your subscription first, then restores tanks and history, then refreshes suggestions and community data. If you were Pro, everything comes back exactly as you left it.
