How it works

Simple to start.
Powerful inside.

Three steps from first launch to a fully synced, insight-aware aquarium companion.

1

Everything saves locally first

Log parameters, add livestock, run the app entirely offline. All data is stored on-device instantly — no internet required, ever.

2

Sign in to unlock the cloud

Google Sign-In links your account to Firebase and Supabase. Streaks, community, and all your tank data sync automatically.

3

New device — instant restore

Sign in with the same Google account. Tankly pulls your tanks, parameters, and history from the cloud in seconds.
