Permanently remove your Tankly account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

How to delete from the app

1
Open the Profile tab Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar
2
Tap Edit Profile Find the Edit Profile option in your profile settings
3
Scroll to Danger Zone Scroll down to the bottom of the Edit Profile screen
Bottom
4
Tap Delete Account Review what will be deleted and confirm your decision

All Tank Data Tanks, livestock, equipment and parameter logs
💬
Community Posts & Comments All posts, replies and interactions

Cloud Backup Data All synced data removed from Firebase
🔗
Google Account Link OAuth connection removed permanently

Deletion is immediate and permanent. There is no recovery period. Export your data via Backup & Sync → Export CSV before deleting if you want to keep your records.
