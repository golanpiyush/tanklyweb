Account · Data deletion

Delete your
Tankly account.

You can request account deletion here or directly inside the app. Either way, your data is removed from our servers permanently and immediately.

⚠️
This cannot be undone. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, all community posts, uploaded photos, and your authentication record from our servers. Your local tank data on your device is not affected — only cloud data is removed.
Fastest way — inside the app
1
Open SettingsTap your profile icon → Settings in the bottom tab bar.
2
Go to AccountScroll to Account section and tap "Account settings".
3
Tap Delete AccountConfirm the deletion. Your account is removed immediately.
Request deletion via web
Fill in your details and we'll process the deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email.
Changed your mind? Go back to Tankly →
💡

Prefer to do it in the app?

Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete account for an instant, no-wait deletion. The app removes all server data immediately upon confirmation.
